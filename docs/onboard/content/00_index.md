# Welcome to SAFARI

> Your complete guide to the SAFARI wildlife detection platform — from first login to API integration.

SAFARI is a complete platform for building, training, and deploying wildlife detection models. Whether you're labeling camera trap images, training a species classifier, or processing video from the field, this documentation covers every feature.

**New here?** Start with [Getting Started](01_getting-started.html) to create your first project and upload data.

---

## User Guide

- **Getting Started** — Create your first project and upload data
- **Image Labeling** — Draw bounding boxes and manage annotations
- **Video Labeling** — Navigate frames, mark keyframes, and annotate video
- **Autolabeling** — Let AI pre-label your datasets with SAM3 or YOLO
- **Training** — Train detection and classification models on your data
- **Model Playground** — Test trained models on new images and video
- **API** — Programmatic access for batch processing and desktop integration
