# Creating Datasets

Datasets are collections of images and videos that you want to analyze.

## Uploading Media

1. Navigate to **Datasets** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Dataset** and give it a name
3. Drag and drop your files or click **Upload**

Supported formats:

| Type | Formats |
|------|---------|
| Images | `.jpg`, `.png`, `.bmp`, `.tiff` |
| Videos | `.mp4`, `.avi`, `.mov` |

## Organizing Your Data

Use descriptive dataset names that reflect the location or purpose:

```
Serra da Estrela — Camera Trap 01
Alentejo — Drone Survey March 2026
```

> **Tip:** You can upload hundreds of files at once — the platform handles batching automatically.
